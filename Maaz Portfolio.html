How It Works

My Process

From first conversation to launch-ready wiki — here's how I work.

01
Discovery Call
I learn your game's content — gameplay systems, lore scope, writing team size, and launch timeline.
02
Architecture Plan
I map the full page hierarchy and category tree, delivered as a document for your review before anything is built.
03
Template Build
I create every infobox and article template on the platform — tested and ready for writers to use immediately.
04
Navigation & Hub Pages
Main page, sidebar, top nav, and category hub pages — the wiki's skeleton is fully live and navigable.
05
Style Guide Delivery
A clear contributor guide so your writers know exactly how to format, tag, and file every type of content.
